Features of iOS
  • Easy to learn: iOS has a stock-like design, which is easy to understand.
  • Homogeneity: Homogeneity ensures comparability of studios and can share model security. Homogeneous standards help build support infrastructure.
  • Operating without trust: Creators don't need to use endorsement to launch studios. The P-layer will fulfill the regulatory function through supervising methods. The P-layer controls, prohibits, and punishes creators' potential fraudulent behavior.
  • The X token: The X token is the core token of iOS, capturing 100% of studio revenue and having governance and ownership of the studio. The sale of X tokens by the creator is considered financing.
